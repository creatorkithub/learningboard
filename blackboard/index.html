Frequently Asked Questions

What are the keyboard shortcuts?

+

You can work faster using these keys:
C: Chalk/Marker
E: Eraser
[ / ]: Decrease or Increase brush size
Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y: Undo / Redo
X: Clear Board
F: Toggle Fullscreen
S: Toggle Sound Plugin

Is LearningBoard really 100% free?

+

Yes! LearningBoard is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium subscription tiers, and no irritating account registrations required. Just open the page and start teaching or drawing instantly.

Does LearningBoard work on iPads and drawing tablets?

+

Absolutely. LearningBoard is deeply optimized for touch devices and hardware styluses like the Apple Pencil or Wacom tablets. It dynamically reads your native stylus pressure to map realistic chalk or marker thickness on the fly.

How do I save my drawings?

+

Your work is automatically saved locally to your device as you draw, meaning you won't lose your work if you accidentally refresh the page. To export your board as a high-quality image for sharing, simply use the "Export" button in the digital toolbar.

Can I use this for online tutoring via Zoom or Google Meet?

+

Yes, our digital blackboard is highly recommended for virtual tutoring! Simply share your browser window in your video conferencing application. Because LearningBoard runs smoothly at up to 120 frames per second on the client-side, your students will see perfectly fluid writing.

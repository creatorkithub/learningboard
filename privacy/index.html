Privacy Policy for Learning Board

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

Learning Board ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website located at learningboard.online. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, including children, educators, and creators worldwide.

Because our application runs 100% client-side within your web browser, we do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike traditional platforms, we do not require user accounts, email registrations, or profile creation.

  • No Server Data: Your drawings, text, and voice interactions are processed entirely on your local device. No data is transmitted to our servers.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, locations, or hardware identifiers.

2. Local Storage and Data Persistence

To allow you to resume your work if you accidentally close your browser tab, this web tool utilizes your browser's native Local Storage or IndexedDB.

  • This data stays entirely on your computer or device.
  • We cannot access, view, or recover your saved local blackboard files.
  • You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache or using the "Clear All" function in the app.

3. Global Privacy Compliance

A. Children's Privacy (COPPA - USA)

Learning Board is designed to be completely safe for kids and students. Because we collect absolutely zero personal information from any user, our application is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not track children or market to them.

B. European Union Users (GDPR - EU)

For users residing in the European Economic Area (EEA), Learning Board complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since no personal data is collected, processed, or transferred to any cloud servers, your "Right to be Forgotten" and data autonomy are absolute - simply delete your browser cache to remove all local canvas data.

C. California Users (CCPA / CPRA - USA)

In compliance with California privacy laws, we confirm that we do not sell, share, or monetize any consumer data, as no data is ever gathered by this web application.

4. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Our website may be hosted using third-party static hosting platforms (such as GitHub Pages or Vercel). These hosting providers may automatically log basic network requests (like your IP address accessing the website file) solely to deliver the webpage files securely and prevent server abuse. We do not have access to these server logs.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated timestamp.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy framework, you can contact the developer via our official repository or support channels.

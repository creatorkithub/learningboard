Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Client-Side Processing

Learning Board is built with extreme privacy in mind. Our application runs 100% on the client-side within your browser. We do not transmit, collect, or store any of your drawings, text, or audio data on our servers.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to auto-save your current board state. This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by you. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or using the "Clear Board" function in the app.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any tracking cookies or third-party analytics services that collect your personal information. We might serve application files via standard CDNs.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
